About MAFLIX

MAFLIX is a smart movie recommendation system that helps you discover movies tailored to your tastes.Powered by advanced search techniques including TF-IDF and Rocchio feedback algorithms, MAFLIX not only finds the movies you search for but also suggests similar titles you'll love.

Easily filter by genre or provide feedback on recommendations to improve future results. Start exploring the world of cinema with MAFLIX today!
